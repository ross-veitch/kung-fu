# /deep-research

Conduct a thorough research pass on a topic and deliver findings as an opinionated memo.

## Steps

1. **Form a hypothesis**: What do you expect to find? Write it down before researching.
2. **Map the landscape** (pass 1): Key players, schools of thought, contested vs settled questions.
3. **Go deep** (pass 2): Primary sources, most-cited evidence, expert disagreement.
4. **Evaluate sources**: Incentives, methodology, recency, independence.
5. **Synthesise**: Form a view. What does the evidence support?
6. **Write the memo**: Bottom line first. Key findings. Caveats. Background.

## Output format

**Recommendation / Bottom Line** (1 paragraph)
**Key Findings** (3–5 bullets with sources)
**Important Caveats** (2–3 bullets)
**Background** (full analysis for those who want depth)
**Sources** (cited, with confidence level: Primary / Secondary / Tertiary)
