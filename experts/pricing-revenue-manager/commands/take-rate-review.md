# /take-rate-review

Review and optimize take rate strategy for an OTA or marketplace. Output: take rate health assessment, benchmarks vs comparable OTAs, specific levers to improve take rate without losing volume.

---

## Step 1: Assess current take rate

- **Current take rate**: [X]%
- **GMV**: $[Y]M
- **Revenue**: $[Z]M (GMV × take rate)

**Benchmark**:
- Booking.com: ~15%
- Expedia: ~15-18%
- Airbnb: ~13-15%

**Assessment**: [At market / Below market / Above market]

---

## Step 2: Identify levers

**To increase take rate**:
1. Add value-added services (insurance, cancellation protection) → charge extra
2. Premium placement fees (hotels pay extra for top visibility)
3. Renegotiate supplier commissions (if you have leverage)
4. Dynamic take rate (charge higher take rate for last-minute bookings)

**Risk**: Suppliers disintermediate (go direct) if take rate too high.

---

## Output Example

```markdown
# Take Rate Review

**Current**: 12% take rate, $100M GMV, $12M revenue  
**Benchmark**: Market average 15%

**Opportunity**: +3% take rate = +$3M revenue annually

## Recommended Levers

1. **Add cancellation insurance** (10% attach rate → +1% blended take rate)
2. **Premium placement** (20% of hotels opt in → +0.5% take rate)
3. **Dynamic take rate** (charge 15% for <7 day bookings → +1% blended)

**Projected take rate**: 14.5% (+$2.5M revenue)
```
