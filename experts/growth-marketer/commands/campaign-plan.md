# /campaign-plan

Plan a growth campaign. Output: channel mix recommendation with budget allocation, creative brief, measurement plan, and expected outcomes.

---

## Step 1: Define goal and budget

- **Goal**: [X installs / Y bookings / Z signups]
- **Budget**: $[amount]
- **Timeline**: [duration]
- **Target CAC**: $[X] (based on LTV)

---

## Step 2: Select channels

| Channel | % of Budget | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Google Search | 40% | High intent, proven CAC $45 |
| Meta Retargeting | 25% | Lowest CAC $20, high conversion |
| SEO | 20% | Long-term investment, $15 CAC |
| TikTok (test) | 15% | Experimental, Gen Z audience |

---

## Step 3: Creative brief

**Messaging**: [What's the value prop?]  
**Variants**: Test [social proof vs urgency vs value prop]  
**Format**: [Static image vs video vs carousel]  
**CTA**: [Book Now vs Find Your Stay]

---

## Step 4: Measurement plan

**Success metrics**:
- CAC target: $[X]
- Conversion rate target: [Y]%
- LTV:CAC target: 3:1+

**Track daily**: CAC, conversion rate, spend pacing

---

## Output Example

```markdown
# Campaign Plan: [Goal]

**Budget**: $50K over 30 days  
**Goal**: 1,000 bookings (target CAC $50)

---

| **Total** | **$50K** | **1,000** | **$50** |

---

## Creative Brief

**Headline**: "Book your dream stay in 2 minutes"  
**Test variants**: Social proof ("10K+ happy travelers") vs Urgency ("Limited rooms — book now")

**CTA**: "Find Your Stay"
```
