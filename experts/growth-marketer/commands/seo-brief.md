# /seo-brief

Create an SEO content brief for a target keyword cluster. Output: search intent, recommended structure, competing content to beat, key questions to answer, and internal linking opportunities.

---

## Step 1: Keyword research

**Target keyword**: [primary keyword]  
**Search volume**: [monthly searches]  
**Difficulty**: [Easy/Medium/Hard]  
**Related keywords**: [secondary keywords to include]

---

## Step 2: Search intent

What is the user trying to do?
- **Informational**: Learn something ("how to book cheap flights")
- **Navigational**: Find a specific site ("booking.com login")
- **Commercial**: Research before buying ("best hotels in dubai")
- **Transactional**: Ready to buy ("book flight to dubai")

**Content format**: [Blog post / Landing page / Guide / Tool]

---

## Step 3: Analyze top 10 results

**Common themes**: What do top results cover?  
**Content gaps**: What are they missing?  
**Word count**: Avg [X] words (aim for 10-20% longer)

---

## Step 4: Recommended structure

**H1**: [Primary keyword]  
**H2s**: [Subtopics based on user intent + related keywords]  
**H3s**: [Supporting details]

---

## Output Example

```markdown
# SEO Brief: "best hotels in dubai"

**Search intent**: Commercial (users researching hotels before booking)  
**Volume**: 18,000/month  
**Difficulty**: Medium  
**Format**: Long-form guide (2000+ words)

---

## Recommended Structure

**H1**: Best Hotels in Dubai (2024 Guide)

**H2**: Top 10 Luxury Hotels in Dubai  
[List format with images, brief descriptions, booking links]

**H2**: Best Budget Hotels in Dubai  
[Alternative for price-conscious users]

**H2**: Where to Stay in Dubai (By Neighborhood)  
- Downtown Dubai
- Dubai Marina
- Jumeirah Beach

**H2**: When to Visit Dubai  
[Seasonality, best time to book]

**H2**: FAQs  
- How much does a hotel in Dubai cost?
- Do I need a visa to visit Dubai?
- What's the best area to stay in Dubai?

---

## Content to Beat

1. **TripAdvisor**: Ranks #1, 3500 words, lacks booking integration
2. **Booking.com**: Ranks #2, strong on filters but thin content
3. **Lonely Planet**: Ranks #3, great neighborhood guide

**Opportunity**: Combine TripAdvisor's depth + Booking.com's booking UX + Lonely Planet's local insights.

---

## Internal Linking

- Link to: [Dubai travel guide], [Cheap flights to Dubai], [Things to do in Dubai]
- From: Homepage, [Middle East destinations page]
```
