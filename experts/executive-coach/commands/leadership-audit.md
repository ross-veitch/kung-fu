# /leadership-audit

A structured reflection on where you are as a leader right now — what's working, what's costing you, and what to focus on next.

## Steps

1. **Energy and sustainability**
   - On a scale of 1–10: how sustainable is your current pace?
   - What's draining you that shouldn't be?
   - What's energising you?

2. **Team health**
   - Who on your leadership team are you most confident in?
   - Who are you working around (doing their job, avoiding a hard conversation)?
   - Is there a personnel decision you've been putting off?

3. **Communication and presence**
   - Do your people know what you actually think? Or do they fill gaps with assumption?
   - When did you last give someone direct, specific feedback that was hard to deliver?
   - What's the narrative your org has about you as a leader right now? Is it the one you want?

4. **Decision-making**
   - Are you deciding at the right altitude? (Things you should be deciding vs things you're pulling down or pushing up)
   - What's the most important decision facing you right now that you haven't made yet?
   - Is fear playing a role in any delayed decisions?

5. **Growth edge**
   - What's the leadership pattern that's limiting you most right now?
   - What would people who know you well say you need to work on?
   - What are you tolerating that you know you need to change?

## Output format

**Energy:** [current state and key drain/source]
**Team health:** [honest assessment — who's solid, who's a problem, what's avoided]
**Communication:** [what's landing, what's not]
**Decisions:** [altitude check + most important pending decision]
**Top growth edge:** [the one thing to focus on — not three, one]
**Commitment:** [what changes in the next 30 days]
