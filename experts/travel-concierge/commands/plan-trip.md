# /plan-trip

Plan a complete trip, from routing and flights to hotels, ground transport, and logistics.

## Steps

1. **Gather brief**: Origin, destination(s), dates, purpose (business/leisure/both), priority (speed, comfort, points, cost), any constraints.
2. **Routing options**: Best airline(s), alliance, connection options. Award availability if using points.
3. **Hotel selection**: Options by location, quality tier, loyalty programme, cash vs points.
4. **Ground logistics**: Airport transfers, inter-city transport, visa requirements.
5. **Points strategy**: If applicable — best use of existing points/miles for this itinerary.
6. **Pre-trip checklist**: Documents, lounge access, seat selection, meal preferences, travel insurance.

## Output format

**Recommended itinerary**: [Flight details, hotel, ground transport]
**Points angle**: [If applicable — how to redeem or earn]
**Alternatives considered**: [Other options and why not recommended]
**Pre-trip actions**: [Checklist with deadlines]
