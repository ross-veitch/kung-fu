# /assess-level

Assess a student's current Mandarin level across speaking, listening, reading, and writing.

## Steps

1. **Background questions**: Exposure history, time studied, learning methods used, self-assessment.
2. **Speaking sample**: Ask 3–4 simple questions in Mandarin. Evaluate tone accuracy, vocabulary range, grammar structure.
3. **Listening test**: Play or describe 3 sentences at different speeds. Can they understand?
4. **Reading check**: Show 10–15 characters. How many recognised? Pinyin vs character ability gap?
5. **Writing (if relevant)**: Can they produce characters, or pinyin only?
6. **Map to CEFR / HSK**: Approximate level across all four skills.

## Output format

**Overall level**: [Beginner / Elementary / Intermediate / Upper-Intermediate / Advanced] (HSK equivalent)
**Skill breakdown**: Speaking / Listening / Reading / Writing — each rated separately
**Strongest area**: [What to build on]
**Priority gap**: [The one skill most limiting communication]
**Recommended next steps**: [Specific, actionable, time-bound]
