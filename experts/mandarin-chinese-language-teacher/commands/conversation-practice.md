# /conversation-practice — Run a structured conversation practice session

**Purpose**: Conduct an interactive Mandarin conversation practice session calibrated to the student's level, with real-time correction, vocabulary expansion, and tone feedback.

**When to use**: When the student wants to practice spoken Mandarin fluency in a structured way — not free conversation, but guided dialogue with learning objectives.

---

## Phase 1: Set up the session

### Determine parameters
1. **Student level**: Beginner (HSK 1–2), Intermediate (HSK 3–4), Upper-intermediate (HSK 5–6), Advanced (HSK 7+)
2. **Topic**: Choose or let student choose from contextually relevant scenarios:
   - **Beginner**: Self-introduction, ordering food, asking directions, shopping, daily routine
   - **Intermediate**: Travel planning, describing past events, expressing opinions, workplace situations
   - **Upper-intermediate**: Debating topics, negotiating, explaining complex ideas, storytelling
   - **Advanced**: Business negotiations, abstract discussions, cultural commentary, idiom-rich conversation
3. **Focus skill**: Fluency (minimize interruptions), accuracy (correct frequently), or vocabulary expansion (introduce new words in context)
4. **Duration**: 15, 25, or 40 minutes

### Session structure
- **Warm-up** (2–3 min): Simple questions in Mandarin to activate the language
- **Core practice** (60–70% of time): Guided conversation on the chosen topic
- **Review** (20–25% of time): Error review, new vocabulary summary, pronunciation drills
- **Homework prompt** (1–2 min): What to practice before next session

---

## Phase 2: Warm-up

Ask 3–5 simple questions to activate Mandarin thinking:

**Beginner:**
- 你好！你今天怎么样？(Nǐ hǎo! Nǐ jīntiān zěnmeyàng?)
- 你昨天做了什么？(Nǐ zuótiān zuòle shénme?)
- 今天天气怎么样？(Jīntiān tiānqì zěnmeyàng?)

**Intermediate:**
- 最近忙不忙？在忙什么？(Zuìjìn máng bù máng? Zài máng shénme?)
- 这个周末有什么计划？(Zhège zhōumò yǒu shénme jìhuà?)
- 你最近看了什么有意思的东西？(Nǐ zuìjìn kànle shénme yǒu yìsi de dōngxi?)

**Advanced:**
- 你对最近的[current event]有什么看法？
- 如果你能改变一件事，你会改变什么？

Correct tone errors gently during warm-up. Note recurring issues for focused practice later.

---

## Phase 3: Core conversation practice

### Guided dialogue structure

**Do not** simply have a free conversation. Structure the dialogue to push the student just beyond their comfort zone (i+1):

**Technique 1: Scenario role-play**
Set up a specific scenario and assign roles:
- "You are checking into a hotel in Shanghai. I am the front desk staff. You need to ask about room upgrade options and breakfast times."
- Conduct the dialogue. When the student gets stuck, provide the word or phrase they need (in Mandarin first, then pinyin, then English only if needed).

**Technique 2: Story building**
Start a story and have the student continue:
- Teacher: 昨天我去了一个很奇怪的餐厅... (Yesterday I went to a very strange restaurant...)
- Student continues. Teacher asks follow-up questions to extend the narrative.
- Pushes use of past tense markers (了/过), descriptive vocabulary, and connected speech.

**Technique 3: Opinion exchange**
Present a statement and ask the student to agree, disagree, and explain:
- 有人说学中文最难的是声调，你同意吗？(Some say tones are the hardest part of learning Chinese. Do you agree?)
- Push for structured arguments: 我觉得... 因为... 但是... 所以... (I think... because... but... so...)

**Technique 4: Information gap**
Each person has information the other needs. Student must ask questions to get it:
- "I have the train schedule. You need to find out which train gets you to Hangzhou before 3pm. Ask me."

### Error correction during practice

**For fluency focus**: Correct only errors that impede understanding. Note other errors for the review phase. Do not interrupt flow.

**For accuracy focus**: Correct in real-time using the "recast" method:
- Student: 我昨天去商店买了很多的东西。(acceptable but 的 is unnecessary here)
- Teacher: 对，你买了很多东西。很好！你买了什么？(Recast without 的, then continue)

**For tone errors**: Use the "echo" method:
- Student: 我想买那个 *shǔ* (wrong tone for 书 shū)
- Teacher: 书？(correct tone, with slight emphasis) 你想买什么书？

## Phase 4: Review and vocabulary summary

### Error review (5–8 min)
Collect the top 5 errors from the session and review them:

```
Errors from today's session:

1. Tone: 书 shū (1st tone, not 3rd) — "book"
   Practice: 我想买一本书 (Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yī běn shū)

2. Grammar: 我在中国去了两次 → 我去过中国两次
   Rule: 过 for life experiences, 了 for completed actions

3. Measure word: 一个书 → 一本书
   Rule: 本 is the measure word for books (bound objects)

4. Word choice: 看书 vs 读书
   看书 = read (casual), 读书 = study/read aloud

5. Missing 了: 我昨天买东西 → 我昨天买了东西
   Rule: 了 marks completed actions in the past
```

### New vocabulary summary
List new words introduced during the session:

```
New vocabulary:

1. 打折 dǎzhé — discount (v/n)
   Example: 这个打几折？(What discount is this?)

2. 试一试 shì yi shì — try (it)
   Example: 我可以试一试吗？(Can I try it?)

3. 合适 héshì — suitable/fitting
   Example: 这件衣服很合适。(This clothing fits well.)
```

### Pronunciation drill
Pick the 2–3 hardest sounds from the session and drill:
- Minimal pairs: 书 shū vs 数 shù vs 树 shù
- Tone pairs: 买 mǎi (3rd, buy) vs 卖 mài (4th, sell)
- Repeat 5× each, with feedback

---

## Phase 5: Homework and next session

Assign specific practice tasks:

1. **Anki cards**: Add today's new vocabulary (with audio if possible)
2. **Shadowing**: Listen to 5 min of native Mandarin at student's level and repeat simultaneously (Du Chinese, Mandarin Corner)
3. **Writing**: Write 5 sentences using today's new vocabulary
4. **Tone drill**: Practice the tone pairs identified in today's session (5 min daily)

Suggest the topic for next session based on what emerged today:
> "Your past tense usage is improving. Next session let's work on expressing future plans and hypothetical situations (如果...就...). Think about a trip you'd like to take — we'll plan it in Mandarin."
