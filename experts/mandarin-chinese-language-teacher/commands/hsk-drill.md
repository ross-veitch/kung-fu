# /hsk-drill

A focused drill session targeting HSK vocabulary and grammar. Always in context — never isolated word lists.

---

## Step 1: Clarify scope

**Required:** HSK level (1–9)
**Optional:** Specific topic (travel, business, food, health), specific grammar point to drill, known weak areas
**Simplified or Traditional?** (Default: Simplified)

---

## Step 2: Select vocabulary

Pull 10 high-frequency, high-utility vocabulary items for the specified level and topic. Prioritise:
- Items that appear frequently in authentic texts at this level
- Items with common collocations worth learning
- Items that the learner is likely to need in real conversations or reading

For each item: Chinese + pinyin + English + example sentence (authentic, not constructed, when possible)

---

## Step 3: Select grammar patterns

Pick 5 grammar patterns relevant to this level. For each: show the structure, give 2 examples, identify common errors.
