## Output Example

```markdown
# Market Brief: Saudi Arabia — Travel Booking App Launch

## Market Context

- **Population**: 35M (60% under 30)
- **Vision 2030**: $500B investment in tourism, targeting 100M visitors/year
- **Payment**: mada card penetration 70%, STC Pay growing
- **Opportunity**: Domestic tourism (Saudis exploring their own country), women travelers (underserved)

## Key Players

- **Almosafer**: Market leader (Seera Group), 40% share
- **Wego**: Strong in GCC, 15% share in Saudi
- **Tajawal**: Regional player, 10% share

## Regulatory

- **Company registration**: 2-3 months (can fast-track with MISA approval)
- **Licensing**: Tourism license required (SCTH), payment license if processing locally
- **Foreign ownership**: 100% allowed in tourism sector (Vision 2030 incentive)

## Cultural Considerations

- **Arabic localization critical**: RTL UI, Arabic content, prayer times
- **Women travelers**: Major growth segment (post-2017 reforms) — market to them specifically
- **Ramadan**: Avoid major launches during Ramadan (business slows)

## Recommended Approach

1. **Partner with local payment provider** (mada, STC Pay) for trust
2. **Launch in Riyadh first** (largest market, early adopters)
3. **Focus on domestic tourism** (Saudis traveling within Kingdom — growth segment)
4. **Invest in Arabic localization** (not just translation — full RTL experience)

## Key Risks

- **Competition**: Almosafer has strong brand loyalty
- **Regulatory changes**: Vision 2030 creates opportunities but also uncertainty
- **Payment infrastructure**: Still cash-heavy in some segments
```
