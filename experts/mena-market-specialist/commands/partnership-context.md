## Output Example

```markdown
# Partnership Context: [Saudi Tourism Development Fund — STDF]

## Background

**Type**: Government-linked entity (under Ministry of Tourism, Vision 2030 initiative)

**Purpose**: Finance tourism infrastructure projects (hotels, resorts, experiences)

**Capital**: SAR 10B ($2.7B) fund to support tourism development

---

## Decision-Making

**Who decides**:
- **CEO**: [Name] (reports to Ministry of Tourism)
- **Board**: 7 members (mix of government officials + private sector experts)
- **Approval levels**: Projects >$50M require Ministry of Tourism approval

**Timeline**: 6-12 months from initial pitch to funding approval (due diligence is thorough)

---

## Key Stakeholders

- **CEO [Name]**: Final decision-maker for projects <$50M
- **CFO [Name]**: Leads financial due diligence
- **Ministry of Tourism**: Oversight, approves large projects
- **PIF (Public Investment Fund)**: Strategic guidance (STDF is aligned with PIF's Vision 2030 mandate)

---

## What They Respond To

**Strategic fit with Vision 2030**:
- Tourism transformation (domestic + international)
- Job creation (especially for Saudi nationals — Nitaqat compliance)
- Technology/innovation (digital tourism, AI/ML applications)
- Sustainability (eco-tourism, carbon-neutral projects)

**Financial structure**:
- Prefer co-investment (not 100% funding — want private sector skin in the game)
- Loan terms: 5-10 year tenor, 3-5% interest
- Equity: Open to equity stakes in high-potential projects

**Relationship**:
- Warm introduction through Ministry of Tourism or PIF-connected advisor is critical
- Cold outreach = low response rate

**Timing**:
- Currently active (Vision 2030 deadline creates urgency)
- Focus on projects that can launch by 2027-2028 (ahead of 2030 target)

---

## Recommended Approach

1. **Get warm introduction** via [Ministry contact / PIF-connected advisor / existing STDF-funded project]
2. **Emphasize Vision 2030 alignment** (tourism jobs, Saudi workforce, domestic tourism)
3. **Highlight co-investment** (STDF funds 50%, we fund 50% → de-risks for them)
4. **Show pilot success** (if possible, prove concept before asking for large funding)
5. **Timeline**: Expect 6-12 months from pitch to funding approval (plan accordingly)

---

## Red Flags

- **Government approval risk**: Projects >$50M require Ministry sign-off (adds 3-6 months)
- **Nitaqat compliance**: Must commit to hiring X% Saudi nationals (non-negotiable)
- **Bureaucracy**: Government-linked entities move slower than private companies
```
