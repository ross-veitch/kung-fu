# /market-entry

Assess market entry strategy for a new geography or segment in travel. Output: market sizing, competitive map, recommended entry approach, key partnerships needed, and success metrics.

---

## Step 1: Market sizing

- **TAM** (Total Addressable Market): $[X]B annual travel spend
- **SAM** (Serviceable Available Market): $[Y]B (online booking segment)
- **SOM** (Serviceable Obtainable Market): $[Z]M (realistic 3-year target at 2-5% share)

---

## Step 2: Competitive landscape

**Incumbents**:
- Player A: 30% market share, strong brand
- Player B: 20% market share, price leader
- Player C: 15% market share, niche (luxury)

**Whitespace**: [Underserved segment or geography]

---

## Step 3: Entry strategy

**Option A**: Organic (build from scratch) → Low cost, slow  
**Option B**: Partnership (integrate with local player) → Medium cost, medium speed  
**Option C**: Acquisition (buy a local player) → High cost, fast

**Recommendation**: [Option] because [rationale]

---

## Output Example

```markdown
# Market Entry: [Target Market]

**TAM**: $10B, **SAM**: $3B (online), **SOM**: $150M (5% share in 3 years)

## Competitive Map

- Incumbent A: 30% share, strong direct brand
- Incumbent B: 20% share, OTA-dominant
- **Whitespace**: Budget travelers underserved (80% market uses premium OTAs)

## Recommended Approach

**Strategy**: Partnership with local OTA [Name] → faster entry, lower risk  
**Investment**: $5M (partnership fee + marketing)  
**Timeline**: 18 months to 5% share  
**Success metrics**: 50K bookings/month by Month 18
```
